Privacy Policy for Esther Vault

Effective date: 2025-03-01

Esther Vault ("Esther Vault", "we", "us", or "our") provides a journaling and audio transcription application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information when you use the App:

Information you provide

  • Account and authentication data: email address, phone number, or Google account information used to sign in.
  • User content: journal entries, audio recordings, and related notes or tags you create in the App.
  • Support communications: messages you send to us for help or feedback.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app information: device type, operating system version, app version, and unique device identifiers.
  • Usage data: screens viewed, features used, and crash or performance diagnostics.
  • Push notification data: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens and delivery status.

Information from third parties

  • Authentication providers: when you sign in with Google or phone OTP, we receive confirmation of your identity from Firebase Authentication.
  • Transcription services: audio recordings are sent to OpenAI Whisper for transcription, and the resulting text is returned to the App.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App and its features.
  • Authenticate users and secure access to accounts.
  • Transcribe audio recordings into text.
  • Send push notifications and in-app reminders you request.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and user experience.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate important updates.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: we use trusted third-party vendors (such as Firebase and OpenAI) to provide authentication, notifications, hosting, analytics, and transcription services.
  • Legal or safety reasons: we may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, safety, and security of users and the App.
  • With your consent: we may share information when you direct us to do so.

Data Retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the App. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us (see "Contact Us").

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices and Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your information. You can also opt out of non-essential notifications in your device settings.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live, including the United States. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy in the App or on our website and update the effective date above.
